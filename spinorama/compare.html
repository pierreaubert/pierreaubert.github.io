Quick help

Graphs are dynamic.

  • Select the two measurements you want to compare.
  • Legend: click on one legend to highlight it. Click on Measurements to be back to default.
  • Over in the graph and you will see a popup with informations.
  • Zoom in and out with your mouse (middle button).
  • Pan left and right with your mouse (left button).
  • Double click on the graph to be back to default.
  • Circled with ... on the upper right is a menu that allow you to editthe graph or to print it.
